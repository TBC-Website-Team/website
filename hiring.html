Hiring the building

Space to hire in the heart of Thatcham

Our rooms are available to hire for community groups, meetings, classes and events. If you're looking for a warm, well-kept space, we'd love to hear from you.

Our spaces

Rooms for all kinds of gatherings

From smaller meeting rooms to our larger hall, our building can suit a range of groups and activities.

  • A range of room sizesSuitable for classes, clubs, meetings, rehearsals and celebrations.
  • Kitchen & refreshment facilitiesHandy for events where you'd like to offer tea, coffee or catering.
  • Accessible & on-site parkingStep-free access and parking, close to the station.

Exact rooms, capacities and hire rates will be listed here — please ask when you enquire.
