Giving

Thank you for your generosity

We don't expect you to contribute — please never feel you have to. But if you'd like to support God's work here in Thatcham, we're genuinely grateful. Every gift, large or small, helps us love our community well.

Your giving keeps the lights on, supports our staff and youth work, funds the foodbank and outreach, and helps us be a place of welcome for everyone in our town. Thank you for being part of that.

In person, at church

There's a giving box at the back of church. Pop in a note, coins or an envelope any time you visit — no fuss, entirely private.

If you're a UK taxpayer, ask about Gift Aid — it adds 25% to your gift at no cost to you.

Online, any time

Give securely online through JustGiving — a one-off gift or a regular amount, whatever suits you. It takes less than a minute.

More than money

Giving your time counts too

From serving coffee to helping at the foodbank or welcoming on the door, there are lots of ways to get stuck in. If you'd like to help, we'd love to hear from you.
