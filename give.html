Give

Thank you for your generosity

We don't expect you to contribute — but if you'd like to support God's work here in Thatcham, we're very grateful. However you give, and whatever the amount, it makes a real difference.

Ways to give

Whatever's easiest for you

There's never any pressure, and you never need to give to belong. If you'd like to, here are two simple ways.

In church

There's a giving box at the back of church. Pop in a gift whenever you'd like to — cash or an envelope, whatever suits. If you're a UK taxpayer, ask about Gift Aid so we can claim a little extra at no cost to you.

Online

Prefer to give from home, or set up a regular gift? You can give securely online through JustGiving in just a couple of minutes.

Where does it go?

Your giving helps us keep the doors open and the welcome warm — supporting our ministry to all ages, our work in the community like the foodbank and refugee support, and the day-to-day running of the church. Thank you.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give… for God loves a cheerful giver.”
