Join us on Sundays

One warm gathering, every Sunday morning

Modern, informal worship for all ages — and it's livestreamed on YouTube, so you can join from home too. Children and young people are cared for and welcome throughout the morning.

Every Sunday

10:30am

Morning Worship

Come as you are — no dress code, no pressure. Little ones and children are looked after so you can relax and settle in. Around an hour and a quarter, with tea and coffee afterwards.

  • Crèche — little ones
  • JAM — primary, up to Year 5

Church is the people

We're the people, not the building

You'll find every stage of life here — from children finding their feet to friends in their 90s with a lifetime of stories. What holds us together isn't a building; it's each other, and the God who welcomes us all.

We're an ordinary, imperfect, wonderfully mixed bunch — families, singles, students, retirees, the curious and the committed. There's room for you exactly as you are.

Loving our community

Faith with its sleeves rolled up

We believe love is something you do. Here's a little of how we try to serve Thatcham and beyond.

West Berkshire Foodbank

A donation basket sits in our foyer all week, and we're a distribution point every Thursday 1–3pm — practical help for neighbours who need it.

West Berkshire Refugee Support

We partner with West Berkshire Refugee Support to welcome and stand alongside those who've had to start life again far from home.

Link to Hope Shoeboxes

Each year we fill shoeboxes with gifts and essentials for families and elderly people across Eastern Europe. A little box, a big hello.
