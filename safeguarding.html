Safeguarding

Keeping everyone safe

The safety and wellbeing of children, young people and adults at risk is a responsibility we take seriously. Everyone who comes to TBC should feel safe, respected and cared for.

If you're worried about the safety or welfare of a child, young person or an adult at risk — whether it relates to something at church or elsewhere — please don't keep it to yourself. Speak to one of our Designated Persons below.

In an emergency, or if someone is in immediate danger, always call 999. You can also contact the NSPCC on 0808 800 5000 or the local authority safeguarding team.
